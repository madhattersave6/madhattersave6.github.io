~ Transistor OST (Vinyl) ~

By popular demand, we're pleased to offer this deluxe double LP set of our award-winning
original soundtrack by Darren Korb with vocals by Ashley Barrett. It features the latest single She Shines,
along with all the other tracks from the soundtrack's first debut.

This new album comes in a glossy old-style gatefold jacket featuring
artwork by Jen Zee and lyrics by Darren Korb. Each record is pressed
on 180 gram milky clear vinyl. Also included is a download code for a digital version
of the track list plus a digital version of the Extended version of the soundtrack.

We collaborated with Pirates Press to create this set and couldn't be happier
with how it all turned out. There's something special about hearing this music on vinyl,
as it fits the feel of the world of Transistor better than any other format.

Limit 2 per person.
