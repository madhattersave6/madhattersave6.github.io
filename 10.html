~ Transistor Sheet Music ~

We're pleased to offer the official sheet music for the songs from the Transistor Original Soundtrack
as a free gift for Transistor's musically-inclined fans. The file contains nine pieces of original sheet music in PDF format,
featuring the following songs written by our award-winning composer and audio director, Darren Korb:

~ "The Spine"
~ "In Circles"
~ "We All Become"
~ "Signals"
~ "Paper Boats"

Most songs have sheet music for both guitar and piano, complete with lyrics and vocal arrangements.
We hope you enjoy it, and thank you again for all your support of our game and its musical score!

Please note: Your shipping info is not required to redeem the sheet music, just a name and email address!
